CHICKEN KARAHI RECIPE

Chicken karahi is a poultry dish that is popular in Pakistan and North India. The word karahi in its name refers to a thick
and deep cooking-pot similar to a wok in which the dish is prepared.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken meet
  • tomato
  • chilies
  • onion
  • butter
  • cilantro
  • lemon
  • ginger
  • garlic

PREPARATION

  1. In a wok fry chicken in oil or ghee with salt and pepper for 2-3 minutes until color changes.
  2. Color has changed and few golden marks are visible. Remove the chicken with a slotted spoon leaving oil in the pan.
  3. In the same wok, fry finely chopped onions for 3 minutes until onion is translucent.
  4. Add the spices and for a minute until spices sizzle.
  5. Add tomatoes pureed or sliced and cook for 3-5 minutes. Cover the wok.
  6. Cook until oil separates and the color of gravy darkens, keep stirring on medium-high heat.
  7. add fried chicken with juices in the wok again. Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes on low flame until chicken is tender.
  8. Add a dollop of butter for a makhni flavor. Garnish with ginger and chili.
  9. Chicken Karahi is ready.

What to serve with

  • Tandoori naan
  • roughni naan
  • pita bread
  • salad
  • rinsed sliced onions
